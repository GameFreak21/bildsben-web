About Me

Hey, my name is Ben. I am currently 14 years old and I live in The United Kingdom (Wales)! I started learning to code when I was about 10 years old. In primary school I learned how to make simple games with Scratch, and was a wanna be hacker. I learned really fast and I needed a new challenge. I looked up "python crash course" on google and I found TechWithTim. He really got me into development, so I thank him for that! I worked with Python2 and Python3 for a long time, but after some time I lost interest...


Fast forward a few years to the age of 14, and I started to want to get paid. I used an app called UniTaskr to find my first "client". I offered to do some ghost writing for him. Pretty fun considering I enjoy learning stuff. I still do work for him! I would consider myself pretty good at writing of all forms. I take Media Studies GCSEs, meaning I am quite used to analysing pieces in depth. I take commissions for my work now, so if you want to get me writing something, email me! You can email me here! (benvoisey[at]mail[dot]ru).


My school decided to do GCSEs a little differently for my year group. I chose my GCSE topics in Year 8 (Grade 7 for you Americans). Therefore, I was forced to choose my future career at an early age. I decided to go back to my roots of using a computer. I always wanted to do ICT or computer science in school, so I took CS! We mainly study python in those classes, so I consider that the language I am most proficient at, however I do know some C#. I also take comissions for these projects! You can email me here!
